#TITLE {cmdname}
#DESCR Usage: `{cfg.prefix}{cmdname} <name>`
#ARGUMENTS
`<name>`
	The name of the player to get stats for. **NOTE**: Quotes will still be evaluated and must be escaped accordingly. For example, to give the name `!s]DarkFire'`, you must escape the single quote: `!s]DarkFire\'` OR `"!s]DarkFire'"`.
#OVERVIEW
Sends an embed containing information about the player name `<name>` using SauerTracker's API. SauerTracker: {cmd.url}
